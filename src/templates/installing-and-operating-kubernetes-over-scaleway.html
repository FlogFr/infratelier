Installing and Operating Kubernetes over Scaleway for less than 100 euros a month

Requirements

Before all projects, we need to look at the requirements. We currently manage around 20 websites with different requirements in terms of technologies at infratelier. We have automized the provisioning of these websites on top of VMs. Most of them are hosted by Hetzner, and couple by Scaleway. This costs around 80 euros a month. To optimize the resource utilization, we decided to install and operate a Kubernetes cluster. The two main requirements for the kubernetes cluster is to scale up with additional worker nodes, and also have all the network encrypted.

If you look around in the hosting provider world for cheap dedicated server, there was a small revolution with Scaleway. It provides cheap and fast dedicated server with good network bandwith. We decided to go with two C2L instances for the Kubernetes nodes, and one C2M for the Kubernetes master, for a total of ~70 euros a month.
